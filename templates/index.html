Poultry Disease Prediction

The Poultry Disease Predictor is a Computer Vision based Ai platform that automatically assesses the Disease of Your Live Stock by analysing the fecal matter images taken from the farm floor

Product Capabilities

  • User uploaded images are instantly processed using an Ai engine and and a report detailing the Disease, a simple explanation obout the disease, Treatments and medical instructions
  • The advanced Ai algorithm let you decide which disease has been diagnosed to the live stock.
  • The data is saved in a database for future model improvements.
  • By following the Report a farmer can start the treatments or take measures accordingly.

A state of the art Computer Vision based customized algorithm was trained to classify Three Live Stock Diseases with and can determine if healthy or not. A fullyy detailed report generated from the images can then be used by the farmers to do the treatments or action required.
